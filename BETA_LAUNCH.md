### 5. Beta Metrics — What Success Looks Like

**Not measured**:
- ❌ Daily active users (DAU)
- ❌ Engagement rate
- ❌ Viral coefficient
- ❌ Time on site

**Measured instead**:
- ✅ **Vault retention** — % of users who post to Vault for 7+ consecutive days
- ✅ **Archetype resonance** — % who change archetype in first week (experimentation) vs. stay with choice (commitment)
- ✅ **Shadow integration** — Ratio of Integrate vs. Burn in Shadow rituals
- ✅ **Circle progression** — % who release from Vault → Family/Work → Outer
- ✅ **Evening Review completion** — % who complete weekly reflections
- ✅ **Profile NFT minting** — On-chain commitment indicator
- ✅ **Qualitative feedback** — Written reflections in Evening Review

**Success definition**:
> "50+ souls who use the Vault daily for 30+ days, complete at least 2 Evening Reviews,
> and report that SovereignRealm has made them feel more sovereign over their digital life."
