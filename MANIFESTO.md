## Your Data Belongs to You First

Your data does not touch a server until you consciously open the gate.

Your thoughts are born in the **Vault Only** — encrypted by the Web Crypto API, addressed by immutable CIDs, stored in the browser's own citadel of IndexedDB and OPFS. No hubs. No relays. No cloud. No compromise.

This is the **purest expression of local-first** in the entire Web3 social pantheon because it refuses every shadow the others still carry:

- **Where others make every post public-by-default on the chain**, SovereignRealm makes every post **private-by-default** — guarded by four sovereign Circles you alone command.

- **Where others externalize identity into visible NFTs and graphs from the first moment**, SovereignRealm lets your Ethereum wallet be your Self, with the optional SovereignProfile NFT minted only when the inner work is done.

- **Where others chase scale through perpetual broadcast**, SovereignRealm practices **Stoic containment**: examine, refine, then release — to Family Realm, Work Collegium, or the federated Outer World via optional ActivityPub.
